About Raja H. Seggoju

I photograph the quiet geometry of urban spaces—the moments where light, shadow, and structure converge into something worth pausing for. My work explores architecture not as backdrop, but as subject: the negative space between buildings, the rhythm of windows, the way concrete ages.

As a professional photographer based in Berlin, I specialize in capturing authentic moments and creative visions. Whether you're looking for family portraits, corporate headshots, event coverage, or artistic portrait sessions, I bring a thoughtful approach to every project.

Photography Services in Berlin

I offer comprehensive photography services throughout Berlin and Brandenburg, working in both English and German. From intimate family photo sessions to large-scale event photography, I'm committed to creating images that tell your story.

Available for: Family portraits (Familienfotografie), Event photography (Eventfotografie), Professional headshots (Businessportraits), Portrait sessions (Portraitfotografie), Creative projects, and Commissioned work.

Based in Berlin, serving all districts including Mitte, Prenzlauer Berg, Kreuzberg, Friedrichshain, Charlottenburg, and surrounding areas in Brandenburg.
